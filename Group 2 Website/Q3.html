STREAMING ANALYSIS

STREAMING

To share our content, streaming type is one of the best ways to spread awareness on social media. We use Facebook to stream our topic, since majority of our friends and relatives use facebook app.It includes many features that are suitable for our stream, cycling.

The level of engagement in the stream (according to the feedback received in the survey) was mostly positive. The viewers really enjoyed the commentary that was provided by the creators, alongside that the commentators and main streamer were able to successfully entertain the audience whilst they were watching the stream. The other method of engaging with the viewers, the chatbox was also utilized in our stream to communicate with the viewers and thankfully, audiences were able to follow suit with the rules that the admin has posted in the chat.

AUDIENCE

our stream got a total of 143 views with 6 shares and 209 comments. As we released our streaming evaluation form. Our audiences shared their feedback and insights. Majority of them find our stream excellent and would willing to recommend our page (5 Brakes). Focusing on how cycling can help reduce greenhouse effect. We want to see the effectiveness of our stream. Thus accepting their suggestions and things to improve, such as opening all of our cameras, engaging and interacting with viewer, and to manage our technical difficulties.

As a group, we also think that our stream has its own cons/ disadvantages such as low signal, audio issues, and technical difficulties since the biking will take place on live. Our group did manage issues properly. We remained calm and continue with our stream. In case of any difficulties we tried our best to keep on entertaining viewers such as telling jokes, asking questions, and telling short stories that are somehow connected to our topic.

About Us

Our main purpose is to give information and ideas to viewers on how cycling can help in reducing the greenhouse effect. We wanted to share our ideas and the reason why we came up with our topic. Knowing The greenhouse effect does a lot of damage to our environment. And as students, we must somehow share our thoughts and skills to help the earth.
